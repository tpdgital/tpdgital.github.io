Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Jiggle Timer application (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by TP Digital (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to respect your privacy. All timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. The Service Provider does not collect, read, or store your personal usage data on any external servers.

However, the Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob

2. Advertising and Consent (GDPR & US State Laws)

The free version of the Application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To comply with global privacy regulations (such as GDPR in Europe and CCPA in the US), we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage your advertising preferences.

  • Personalized Ads: AdMob may use device identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID) and cookies to serve ads based on your interests.
  • Consent Management: Users in regulated regions will be presented with a consent form upon first launch to opt-in or opt-out of data collection for advertising purposes.
  • Right to Revoke: You may withdraw or change your consent at any time via the "Privacy & Cookie Settings" link found within the Application's Settings menu.

3. Required System Permissions

To ensure alarm reliability across various device manufacturers, the Application requests specific permissions:

  • Battery Optimization Exemption: Allows the app to trigger alarms precisely even when the device is in power-saving mode.
  • System Alert Window: Required to wake the screen and show the alarm interface when the phone is locked.
  • Vibration: Used for tactile feedback for the "ticking" and final alarm.

4. In-App Purchases and Billing

The Application offers a "Premium Upgrade" to remove advertisements.

  • All payments are securely processed and managed by Google Play Billing.
  • The Service Provider does not collect or store your credit card information or financial details. We only receive an anonymous digital receipt from Google to unlock premium features.

5. Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If it is discovered that such data has been provided, it will be immediately deleted.

6. Security

The Service Provider implements standard electronic and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality of any information processed within the app.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. You are advised to consult this policy regularly for any changes.
